# Contributing to Nhan Kiet To's Portfolio

Thank you for your interest! However, this is a **personal portfolio** and is not open for external contributions.

## Why?

This portfolio represents my personal brand, work history, and projects. Accepting external contributions could:

- Misrepresent my skills or experience
- Introduce content I haven't personally vetted
- Create legal/licensing complications
